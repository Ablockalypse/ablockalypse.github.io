Welcome to Ablockalypse!

Explore a world where strategy meets instinct. Build, and conquer the lines in this block-based challenge.

Features:

  • Keyboard and Gamepad compatable
  • Dynamic size playing area.
  • Designed from the ground up for competitive play.
  • Configurable Input Delay rate and Repeat rates.
  • Wall kick.
  • The encouraging music will keep you hooked and the beautiful backgrounds will leave you wanting more.

Roadmap:

  • Block Editor - Edit the shapes of the blocks
  • Simplified Chineese
  • Career Mode
  • Adventure Mode - A top secret way of playing a tetromino game, never seen before!
  • A match 3 style mode.
  • More Music and more varied types of music.
  • Two Player Head to Head.
  • More polish.
    • Some particle effects when lines are cleared and also when a block is set into place.
    • Odometer Style score that spins up when points are added.
    • An animated game over text that moves in the shape of a sin wave.
  • A few more sound effects
    • When a combo is made
    • Possibly when a level is completed, although the background change is probably enough. What do you think?
Would you like to discuss more? Do you have some ideas you would like us to try? Please find a link to our discord server at the bottom of this page.
